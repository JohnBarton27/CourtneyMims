About Courtney

Meet Courtney Mims.

Courtney Mims is a second year telecommunications student at the University of Florida. She has worked previously with Sports Radio 1560 "The Fan" with Cumulus Media in Melbourne as an intern for the Mark Moses Show. She is currently working as a multi-media journalist for WUFT's 89.1 and a Gators Football/basketball beat reporter for College Primetime.

On-Camera Work

Everyday Gator

Everyday Gator is a web series produced on the campus of the University of Florida and in the Gainesville community by Tangible Productions. Courtney has hosted numerous Everyday Gator segments since Fall 2015.

SG Success Series

In April 2016, Courtney Mims co-hosted a series of videos promoting the success of Student Government over the 2015-16 academic year. She interviewed top members of Student Government for the video series, including Student Body President Joselin Padron-Rasines, Student Body Treasurer Nicholas Carre, and many others.
